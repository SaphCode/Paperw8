Story

In July of 2018, Chris and I each invested $1000 in Regi at an average price of roughly $16.50 per share. By September of the same year the stock had surged to $27. We sold our position. Our initial $1000 had turned into $1600 over the course of just three months. Making money is that easy...?

In retrospect

Now, four years and a dozen investing books later, I don't think we would have made the same decision again. Nevertheless, I don't now if I would have ever gotten interested in the stock market if it wasn't for this lucky pick.

Thanks for showing me this fascinating world Chris!
